Hide Like-Dislike Ratio On Profile:

ⓘ

Turning this off will make it so that the like to dislike chart no longer appears on your profile page (Note: this chart is only able to be seen by you and nobody else can see your own personal like-dislike ratio).

Age Range:

ⓘ

This is the age range of other dogs that will appear on your like page. The larger your age range is, the more likely you are to find potential matches! (Note: changing your age rage will not affect your matches).

Interests:

ⓘ

Selecting interests will allow you to find users that share your similar interests! You can choose up to 3 interests.
